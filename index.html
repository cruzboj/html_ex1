About Chicken Jockey

Chicken jockeys are rare mobs in Minecraft where a baby zombie rides a chicken. They're fast, small, and hard to hit, making them surprisingly dangerous despite their funny appearance.

They spawn rarely, which makes seeing one both exciting and a little scary. The chicken lets the zombie move quickly and dodge obstacles easily.
